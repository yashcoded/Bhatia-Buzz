# Bhatia Buzz – Safety Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

> Last updated: 9 March 2026  
> Contact: **safety@yashcoded.com**

Bhatia Buzz is strictly committed to protecting children and preventing all forms of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We do not tolerate any content, behaviour, or activity that harms or risks harming children.

This page explains our standards, how we enforce them, and how users can report concerns.

---

## 1. Zero‑tolerance policy

We operate a **zero‑tolerance policy** towards CSAE. This means:

- We **do not permit** any content that:
  - Depicts, promotes, justifies, or attempts to sexualise minors.
  - Involves nudity, partially clothed, or sexualised images of real or perceived minors.
  - Describes or encourages sexual acts involving minors.
  - Links to, or helps others to access, CSAE material on other services.
- We **do not permit** any behaviour that:
  - Attempts to groom, lure, or coerce minors into sexual activity (online or offline).
  - Asks minors to share sexual or intimate images, videos, or personal contact details for sexual purposes.
  - Romanticises or normalises relationships between adults and minors.

Any attempt to use Bhatia Buzz to create, share, request, or distribute CSAE content or to exploit minors will result in **immediate account termination** and, where appropriate, **reporting to relevant authorities**.

---

## 2. User‑generated content and reporting

Bhatia Buzz includes user‑generated content such as posts, requests, profiles, and messages. To help keep the community safe:

- Users must **not** upload, post, or share:
  - CSAE material or anything that sexualises minors.
  - Jokes, memes, or “role‑play” that involve minors in sexual scenarios.
  - Content that asks for sexual images or favours from minors.
- Users are encouraged to **report** any content or behaviour they believe may involve CSAE.

### How to report

If you see something suspicious, harmful, or that may involve CSAE:

1. Use the in‑app reporting tools where available (for posts, profiles, or requests).  
2. Or email us directly at **safety@yashcoded.com** with:
   - A short description of what you saw.
   - Screenshots if it is safe and legal to capture them.
   - The approximate date/time and any usernames or profile links.

We review all CSAE‑related reports as a priority.

---

## 3. Moderation and enforcement

When we become aware of potential CSAE content or behaviour, we may take one or more of the following actions:

- **Immediate removal** of the content from the app.
- **Suspension or permanent termination** of the involved accounts.
- **Blocking of devices or identifiers** to prevent repeat abuse.
- **Reporting to relevant authorities or hotlines**, where required and appropriate, in line with applicable laws.

We also reserve the right to:

- Disable certain features for specific users or regions if we believe this is necessary to protect minors.
- Prevent previously banned users from returning to the service.

---

## 4. Protection of minors on the platform

Bhatia Buzz is designed for adults in the community and is **not targeted at children**. To further protect minors:

- Users must **not misrepresent their age** in order to access age‑restricted features.
- Users must **not**:
  - Target minors with romantic or sexual messages.
  - Attempt to move conversations with minors to private channels (e.g. other apps) for sexual purposes.
  - Request images, videos, or personal contact details from minors for sexual reasons.

We recommend that parents and guardians:

- Supervise minors’ use of internet‑connected devices.  
- Talk to children about online safety and how to report inappropriate behaviour.  
- Contact us at **safety@yashcoded.com** if they believe a minor’s safety has been impacted by activity on Bhatia Buzz.

---

## 5. Cooperation with authorities and other organisations

Where required by law, or where we believe a child is at risk of serious harm, we may cooperate with:

- Law enforcement agencies.
- Child protection organisations and hotlines.
- Other online service providers involved in the same incident.

Co‑operation may include:

- Preserving and sharing relevant account information or content, within the limits of applicable privacy and data‑protection laws.  
- Providing technical information that may help identify the individuals involved.

We will only share information that is legally permitted and necessary to help protect children.

---

## 6. Relationship to our other policies

These CSAE safety standards sit alongside our other legal and policy documents, including:

- **Privacy Policy** – explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data.  
- **Terms of Service** – sets out the rules for using Bhatia Buzz.  
- **Community and Content Guidelines** – explain what is and is not acceptable in posts, requests, and profiles.

Violations of this CSAE policy will typically also be treated as violations of our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
