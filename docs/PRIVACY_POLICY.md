# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** See the in-app Privacy Policy screen for the current date, or update this line when you change the policy.

---

Welcome to **Bhatia Buzz**. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

---

## 1. Introduction

We are committed to transparency about our data practices. This policy applies to the Bhatia Buzz mobile application and any related services. By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

- **Account information** — Email, display name, profile photo
- **Phone number** — Optional, if you choose to provide it
- **Posts and content** — Content you create (e.g. feed posts, condolences, celebrations)
- **Requests** — Condolences, celebrations, and match requests you send or receive
- **Matrimonial profile information** — If you create a matrimonial profile (e.g. preferences, photos, details used for matching)
- **Usage data** — How you interact with the app (e.g. features used, session data)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

- **Provide and maintain our services** — To run the app, authenticate you, and store your content
- **Enable community features** — Feed, requests, and matrimonial matching
- **Communicate with you** — About your account, security, or important updates
- **Ensure app security** — Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
- **Comply with legal obligations** — Where required by law or to protect our rights

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We **do not sell** your personal data. We may share your data with:

- **Other users** — As part of community features (e.g. your profile, posts, or requests as permitted by the app)
- **Service providers** — Such as Firebase (authentication, database, storage), hosting, and analytics, under strict data processing terms
- **Legal authorities** — When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety

---

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA and similar laws)

You have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data
- **Request data portability** — Export your data in a usable format
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Request deletion** of your data
- **Object** to processing of your data
- **Withdraw consent** at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us (see Contact Us below) or use the relevant options in the app (e.g. Settings, data export, account deletion).

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your account credentials private.

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. When you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal, regulatory, or legitimate business purposes (e.g. resolving disputes, enforcing our terms).

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU without parental consent). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

---

## 9. International Transfers & Data Residency

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this policy and applicable law.

**Data storage location:** Your data is stored on Firebase servers located in the United States. We have implemented appropriate safeguards for cross-border data transfers, including standard contractual clauses and technical security measures, to ensure your data is protected in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including UAE Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) and Qatar Law No. 13 of 2016.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the “Last Updated” date. For significant changes, we may also notify you via email or an in-app message. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
